About our setting

We are based inside a Church Hall, and we set up and packaway the preschool each day, as we share the space with other groups.


We have strong relationship with Curdworth Primary School, and often get the opportunities to join in with their events,
and each week they are generous enough to allow us to visit their school to use their resources and space for PE.
